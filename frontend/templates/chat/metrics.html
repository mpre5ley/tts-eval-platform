Understanding the Metrics

Time to First Byte (TTFB)

The time from sending the synthesis request to receiving the first byte of the response. Lower is better.

Time to First Audio (TTFA)

The time until the first playable audio chunk is available. Critical for real-time applications.

Total Latency

The complete duration from request to fully received audio. Includes all processing and transfer time.

Chunk Jitter

The standard deviation of inter-chunk arrival times during streaming. Lower jitter means more consistent delivery and smoother playback.

Real-time Factor (RTF)

Audio duration / synthesis time. Values > 1 mean faster than real-time synthesis.

Percentiles (P50, P95, P99)

Statistical distribution of synthesis times. P95 means 95% of requests are faster than this value.
